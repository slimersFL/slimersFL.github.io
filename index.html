◇ THE STORY ◇

WHO ARE
SLIMERS?

Formed in the gargling bogs of the Everglades, Slimers is a two-piece slimey egg punk duo crawling out of Lake Worth, FL. Somewhere between Ghostbusters fan fiction and a garage sale gone spectacularly wrong.

They play fast, sloppy, and extremely gooey. Their live shows are packed with original skits, costumes, and enough ectoplasm energy to fill a proton pack.

"Ramonescore in a Ghostbusters suit."

THE SOUND

ORIGINAL SKITS

Between songs, between worlds. Slimers' live shows are punctuated by fully original, totally unhinged comedy skits. Ghostbusters lore meets Florida swamp energy. You never know what's coming next.

👻

GHOSTBUSTERS ENERGY

Every show is a haunting. Expect costumes, props, references, and an unmistakable reverence for the greatest paranormal investigation team of all time. Except these ones play bass and drums.

⚡

RAW PUNK POWER

Beneath the slime, it's a proper two-piece punk wrecking crew. Fast songs, loud guitars, sweat, and chaos. No setlist survives contact with a Slimers show intact.
